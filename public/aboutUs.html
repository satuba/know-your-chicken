Know Your Chicken

It's important to know what you're eating! Often there's a wall between consumers and the food they eat and we're here to help take that wall down. Consumers deserve to know what's in the chicken and eggs they eat.

About us

Curious if you're buying the best chicken for your health? What does "natural" or "fresh" actually mean? Some companies can be shady about their products and we're here to add some transparency to the food industry.

What we do

We've done the research so you don't have to! If you're at the store and want to know the deal about a particular brand or if you care about specific aspects, you can search through to find the best brands for you.

More information

Knowledge is power! Check out our information page to learn more about the chicken and eggs you eat and the different characteristics you might want to look out for.
